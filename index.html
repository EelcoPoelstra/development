about me

I build clean, responsive websites that work on a variety of screens and devices and may or may not be powered by WordPress. I work with Adobe, SASS, HTML5/CSS3 & Javascript. I have a working knowledge of popular frameworks and know my way around version management and working in SCRUM teams.
